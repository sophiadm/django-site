WHO ARE WE?

CFS Aero is an EASA145 MRO who repair and overhaul modern piston and turbine engines.
When the company was founded over 25 years ago its focus was on Classic Engines which it now no longer supports.
Accordingly, the company has a large number of parts within its stores which it is selling off to private collectors and commercial operators.

CONDITION OF PARTS

All parts are sold as removed and most are in good condition (some are brand new and in their original packaging).
We will gladly advise you of the visual condition of any part you are interested in.
Most parts can also be overhauled and supplied with a Certificate of Conformity at an additional cost.
